**Mobile Enchantments** is the art of shaping spells that fit the magical tomes carried by every traveler and noble in the realm. With the right incantations, I turn ideas into touchable enchantments—apps that respond to every scroll, tap, and whisper.

To forge these mobile spells, I use a mix of old and new magic. Sometimes I draw upon general-purpose spells from the frontlines like `HTML`, `CSS`, and `JavaScript`, combined with the sacred teachings of `Responsive Design`. Other times, I wield more focused tools of the trade—like the hybrid scrolls of `React Native` and `Expo`.

Though I've also touched the deeper magics of `Swift`,  `Kotlin` and `Flutter`, those paths still demand patience and practice. Yet with each trial, my spellbook grows stronger.
